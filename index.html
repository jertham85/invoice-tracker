Upload invoice PDF

PDF extraction runs through Claude — it can't connect to the AI directly from this page due to browser security restrictions.

To extract an invoice from a PDF:

  1. Open your Claude chat
  2. Upload the PDF file directly in the chat
  3. Say Extract invoice from this PDF
  4. Claude will extract the details and save the invoice to your tracker automatically
You can also manually enter the invoice details using the + New invoice button while you wait.
